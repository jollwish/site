    Postgraduate Student Position

    We are looking for talented and enthusiastic PhD Students who are interested in nanotechnology to join our team. Since the research is interdisciplinary, the background in Chemistry, Material Science and Nanofabrication is preferred. Please send CV and letter of application with research interest directly to Dr. Tang for consideration. Positions are available for candiate with sound background. Immediate enrollment is possible.

    For normal cases, the application is received and assessed after application deadline (December 1st). Short listed applicants will be informed for phone interview or face-to-face interview before oral or official offer is made. We are looking for members with best fit experience and skill-set and positive research attitude for game-changing research.

    A original research proposal is required for application. Applicant should write any topics broadly in the field of nanoscience and nanotechnology that are most familiar and comfortable to applicants and should be originally from applicant. The research proposal template is recommended.

    Special notice for Hong Kong PhD Fellowship applicants

    The Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS) aims at attracting the best and brightest students in the world to pursue their PhD programmes in Hong Kong's institutions by providing competitive package.

    HKPFS Award

    The Fellowship provides an annual stipend of HK$240,000 (approximately US$30,000) and a conference and research-related travel allowance of HK$10,000 (approximately US$1,300) per year to each awardee for a period of up to three years.

    In order to be successful in HKPFS application, your undergraduate GPA should be among top 10% in your institute. If you think you are qualify to HKPFS, please contact Dr. Tang directly for personal assessment as early as possible. Early offer and finacial aid will be made if you are recommended by Dr. Tang regardless of final result of HKPFS application.

    Special notice for applicants interested in self-funded study

    All applicants are considered for addmission with scholarship. However, due to limited resouces and quota, only limited scholarship can be offered. In case you are also interested in self-funded graduate study, please contact with Dr. Tang directly.

    Note, doing so will not affect your application to research graduate scholarship, but only providing an additional possiblities to be accepted if your scholarship applicant is not accepted.

    Undergraduate Student Position

    HKU undergraduate students interested in nanoscience and nanotechnology are welcome to join in our team. Students from all departments and faculties can apply.

    Oversea students who interested in short-term internship, should send your CV directly to Dr. Tang for consideration.
